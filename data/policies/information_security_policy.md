# Information Security Policy

## Password Requirements

Passwords must contain at least 12 characters and include a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

## Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all company systems.

## Phishing Reporting

Employees must report suspected phishing emails to the IT Security Team within 24 hours.

## Device Security

Company devices must automatically lock after 10 minutes of inactivity.

Employees may not install unauthorized software on company devices.
