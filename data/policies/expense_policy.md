# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## General Guidelines

Business-related expenses may be reimbursed when properly documented.

## Home Office Equipment

Employees may expense one monitor up to $300 every 3 years.

Manager approval is required before purchase.

## Meals

Client meals up to $75 per person are reimbursable.

## Submission Deadline

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.
