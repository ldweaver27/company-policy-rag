# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees of BrightWave Technologies are eligible for paid time off benefits beginning on their first day of employment.

## Annual PTO Allowance

Employees receive 20 days of PTO per calendar year.

PTO may be used for vacation, personal business, or other approved absences.

## Carryover

Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the following calendar year.

Any PTO above 5 days will be forfeited on January 1.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year.

Unused sick leave does not carry over.

## Approval Process

PTO requests of more than three consecutive days must be submitted at least two weeks in advance and approved by the employee's manager.
