# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

Employees may work remotely after completing 90 days of employment.

## Standard Schedule

Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.

Employees are expected to work on-site at least 2 days per week.

## Work Hours

Employees must be available between 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM local time.

## Equipment

Employees are responsible for maintaining a reliable internet connection.

The company provides one laptop and necessary peripherals.
