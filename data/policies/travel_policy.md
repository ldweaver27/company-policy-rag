# Business Travel Policy

## Air Travel

Employees should book economy-class airfare for domestic travel.

Flights exceeding $600 require manager approval.

## Lodging

Hotel accommodations up to $250 per night are reimbursable.

## Ground Transportation

Reasonable taxi, rideshare, and public transportation expenses are reimbursable.

## Submission Requirements

Travel expenses must be submitted within 30 days of trip completion.
